About me

After graduation I started working for a mid-size company in the Plant and Facility Automation industry in Germany as a data scientist. Couple of years later I decided to relocate back to my Bulgarian roots and started to work for a small outsourcing company as a full-stack developer.

At some point I had the opportunity to start my own business, a full-blown SaaS-solution based on a cutting edge technological stack. Due to difficulties in establishing and provisioning for a team, I ultimately decided to cease operation and move on to find a regular full-time job and a team I truly resonate with.
